Spaghetti

A simple and delicious recipe for spaghetti with meat saucea

Ingreients:

  • 1 box of thin spaghetti noodles
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 jar of Classico tomato sauce (your choice)

Steps:

  1. Put ground beef in pan and cook until done, breaking up as it cooks. Add sauce and lower heat once beef is cooked
  2. At the same time, have a large pot of water set on high heat and bring to a boil
  3. Once boiling, add noodles and let cook for 7-8 minutes
  4. Take cooked noodles and pour through strainer
  5. Enjoy!
