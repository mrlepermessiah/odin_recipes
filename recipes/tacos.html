Tacos

Incredible recipe for classic beef tacos at home.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • soft flour tortillas
  • head of lettuce
  • shredded cheese
  • Old El Paso taco seasoning packet
  • taco sauce(your choice)

Steps:

  1. Cook beef in pan until done
  2. Drain excess grease(if you want) and add taco seasoning. Mix well.
  3. Wrap tortillas in papertowel and microwave for 30 seconds
  4. Prepare your tacos and enjoy!
